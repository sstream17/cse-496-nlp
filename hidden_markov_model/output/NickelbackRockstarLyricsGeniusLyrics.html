About “Rockstar”

“Rockstar” is about wanting to live the life as a Rockstar and all its pros but is actually satirical in it’s execution. The band sing about how they have to give up their lives to achieve their goals and how in the end they’ll just be washed up and fake, taking a stab at the mainstream rock industry.
